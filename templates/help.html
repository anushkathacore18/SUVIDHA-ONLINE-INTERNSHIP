About the Portal

Welcome to the official Suvidha Help & Support Portal. This platform is designed to streamline assistance for all users interacting with the Suvidha recruitment ecosystem.


  • Students: Get recruitment-related help, application guidance, and interview support.
  • Employers: Find answers about partnerships, posting roles, and onboarding talent.
  • TPOs: Connect your institution with Suvidha and monitor student placements.

Frequently Asked Questions

For Students

How do I apply for recruitment?

Use the student login to access open roles and submit your application.

Will I get recruitment updates via email?

Yes, all communication will be sent to your registered email ID.

For Employers

How do I post roles for students?

Register as an employer and navigate to your dashboard to post job opportunities.

Do you support virtual hiring?

Yes, we facilitate both virtual and offline recruitment processes.

For TPOs (Training & Placement Officers)

How can I link my college with Suvidha?

Register via the TPO login and await verification by our team.

Can I track student selections?

Yes, you will get access to analytics and student application status.
